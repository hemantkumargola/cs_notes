Advantages of Computer Networks

Resource Sharing – Share printers, files, and internet connections.
Data Sharing – Easy and fast file transfer between devices.
Communication – Supports email, chat, and video conferencing.
Centralized Management – Easier to manage users, data, and security.
Cost Saving – Reduces hardware and software costs through sharing.



Disadvantages of Computer Networks

Security Risks – Vulnerable to hacking, viruses, and malware.
High Setup Cost – Installation and networking equipment can be expensive.
Maintenance Required – Requires regular monitoring and troubleshooting.
Network Failure – If the network fails, communication and resource sharing are affected.
Performance Issues – Heavy network traffic can slow down the network.
